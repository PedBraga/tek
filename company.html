About us

StoneTek is one of the largest importers, manufacturers and installers of natural stone and quartz countertops in New England. Our market coverage includes residential and commercial projects for the wholesale industry.

Convenient and informed, we have what you seek!

We want to be the first to “Welcome You” to our company. Recently, StoneTek moved to a new state of the art building, custom built to accommodate Natural Stone Fabrication with the latest innovative machinery and technology.

Over the years, we have built partnerships with overseas suppliers and local distributors to bring you the widest selection of premium natural stones and quartz products such as Silestone, Hanstone, Zodiaq and more. As the trend toward green products continue, we are pleased to offer you a line of manufactured products using pre- and post-consumer recycled materials, glass and concrete; including eco, IceStone and Curava. We will continue to monitor industry trends aimed at maintaining eco-friendly re-cycle and re-use processes in manufacturing and installation of countertop products.

At StoneTek, we are committed to providing you the best selection, quality and service excellence in the natural stone industry.
